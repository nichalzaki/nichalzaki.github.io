About Nichal

12+ years turning manuscripts into beautifully crafted, platform-ready books.

Nichal Zaki
Book Interior Designer & Layouter · Semarang, Indonesia

I've spent more than a decade obsessing over the space between letters, the rhythm of a paragraph, and the quiet architecture that makes a reader forget they're even reading a book.

I started in traditional print design, working on academic journals and trade publications, before transitioning fully into book interior design. Today I specialize in self-publishing — helping independent authors and small publishers get print-ready, platform-approved files without the back-and-forth of rejections and reformatting.

Every project I take on has one goal: make your book look like it belongs on a shelf next to any traditionally published title. That means precision, patience, and never cutting corners on the details that matter.

How I work

My process

A structured workflow that keeps projects moving without surprises — from your manuscript to approved upload.

01
💬
Discovery
We discuss your book's genre, target audience, trim size, platform, and visual references. I ask questions most designers skip — because they matter.
02
✏️
Design & Layout
I build a custom typographic system and grid, then lay out every page with precision — handling widows, orphans, spacing, and image flow.
03
🔄
Review & Revisions
You receive a proof PDF. We refine together — fonts, spacing, chapter headers, anything that isn't right. Revisions are included, not charged per change.
04
🚀
Export & QA
Final files are preflighted, validated (ePub), and exported to your platform's exact spec. You receive a ready-to-upload package — no guesswork.
Toolchain

Tools I use

Industry-standard software chosen for precision, reliability, and output quality.

Primary Layout
Adobe InDesign
The industry standard for book layout. Used for every interior design project — from novels to full-color coffee table books. Exported as preflight-ready PDFs.
Cover & Illustration
Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator
Photoshop handles photo compositing and CMYK color for print covers. Illustrator builds vector ornaments, infographics, icons, and scalable design elements.
Ebook Production
Sigil & Calibre
Sigil for clean ePub 3 authoring with semantic HTML. Calibre for MOBI/KFX conversion. EPUBCheck for validation — I ship 0-error files every time.
Typography
Glyphs App
Used for custom type design projects — bespoke display fonts, ligature sets, and decorative glyphs that give a book a one-of-a-kind visual identity.
Preflight & QA
Adobe Acrobat Pro
Every PDF is run through Acrobat's preflight engine before delivery — checking bleed, color profiles, resolution, embedded fonts, and platform compliance.
Collaboration
Google Drive & Notion
Files are shared securely via Google Drive. Project notes, revision logs, and timelines are tracked in Notion so we're always on the same page — literally.
How I approach work

What I believe in

🎯
Precision over speed
I would rather take an extra day to get the spacing right than rush a file that fails preflight or looks wrong on the page.
📐
Invisible craft
Great book design doesn't call attention to itself — it simply makes reading feel effortless. The grid, the margins, the type — all in service of the words.
💬
Clear communication
I respond fast, explain my decisions, and never leave you guessing about the status of your project. No radio silence, no vague updates.
🔁
Revisions are part of the work
I don't treat revision requests as interruptions. They're how we get from good to exactly right. Every included round is taken as seriously as the first draft.
📦
Clean file delivery
Every package I deliver is organised, labelled, and ready to use. Print PDFs, ePub files, covers — packaged clearly so you know exactly what you're uploading.
🌏
Built for global publishing
My clients are authors and publishers in the US, Europe, and Australia. I understand the spec requirements of every major platform and deliver accordingly.
